Our focus areas:

Since the youth and children generation has numerous challenges, more than any other group, MYCN focuses on the following focus areas;



Youth and Children rights and policies



Education support



Economic empowerment



Science and Technology

HIV/AIDS and Drug abuse



Reproductive health



Sports and culture



Corruption



Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) and strategies for poverty eradication.
