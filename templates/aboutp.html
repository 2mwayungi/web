ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

Mwanza Youth and Children Network (MYCN) is a youth led NGO. The organization was established to serve the youth and children generation living in the Tanzania mainland through different programs and project interventions.

Our Vision:

A prosperous Tanzania with a youth and child generation that have sustainable development.

Our Mission:

To inspire and facilitate youth and children to have the best means to participate and acquire their intellectual, physical, moral, cultural, and economic development for their own benefits and society in general.

Our Slogan:

HOPE! ACTION! CHANGE! meaning that in any movement towards achieving a certain vision the most import thing to have at the beginning is HOPE!. HOPE! alone is not enough ACTION! should be taken so as to attain the desired CHANGE!.

Our Values:

Integrity and ethics, Volunteerism spirit, Commitment, Hard working, Leadership, Teamwork, Continuous improvement, Communication, Recognition, and Accountability.

Overall Goal:

Youth and children communities are inspired, motivated, involved and supported to enable them realize their roles so they can participate in the developmental processes.
